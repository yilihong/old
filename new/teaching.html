Currently Teaching: Problem Solving and Actionable Analytics

Data Exploration and Visualization is one of the core courses in the analytics curriculum. The course is designed to achieve the following learning goals:

• Understand the key data visualization principles and techniques.
• Apply data visualization principles and techniques to real-world business problems.
• Critically evaluate the design and presentation of complex data or concept.
• Design innovative static and interactive visualizations to effectively present information and enable data exploration.
